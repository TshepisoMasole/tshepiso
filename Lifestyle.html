    Buckingham Palace garden

    The Garden at Buckingham Palace is a large private park attached to the London residence of the monarch. It is situated at the rear of Buckingham Palace

    Westminster Abbey

    A royal church in the centre of London offering daily services for all and a World Heritage Site with over a thousand years of history.

    ‘Big Ben’ clock tower

    Big Ben is the nickname for the Great Bell of the striking clock at the north end of the Palace of Westminster; the name is frequently extended to also refer to the clock and the clock tower
